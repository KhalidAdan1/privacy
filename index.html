Privacy Policy

Better Nights

Last updated: [09 , 09, 2025]

Better Nights (“we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account & Profile Data: Information you provide, such as name, username, email, and any optional profile details.
  • App Content: Data you create or upload in the app (stored in our database to provide core functionality).
  • Usage Data: Basic diagnostics and interaction data (e.g., screens viewed, device type, app version) to improve reliability and performance.
  • Log & Device Data: IP address, device identifiers, operating system, and timestamps when necessary for security and fraud prevention.

Firebase/Firestore: We use Google Firebase (including Firestore) to host and process app data. See Firebase Privacy & Security and Google’s Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the app and its features.
  • Authenticate users and personalize the experience.
  • Protect against fraud, abuse, and security risks.
  • Respond to inquiries and provide support.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Legal Bases (EEA/UK only)

If you are in the EEA/UK, we process personal data under these legal bases: (i) performance of a contract (to provide the app), (ii) legitimate interests (e.g., security, improvement), and (iii) consent where required.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

  • Service Providers: We share data with trusted vendors (e.g., Firebase) who process it for us and under our instructions.
  • Legal & Safety: We may disclose information to comply with law, enforce terms, or protect rights and safety.
  • No selling: We do not sell your personal information.

5. Data Retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary to provide the app and for legitimate business or legal purposes. We delete or anonymize data when it is no longer needed.

6. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access, Update, Delete: You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt-Out of Marketing: If we send emails, you can unsubscribe at any time.
  • EEA/UK: You may object to processing, request restriction, or portability. You can also complain to your local data protection authority.
  • California (CCPA/CPRA): You may request access/deletion and to limit the use of sensitive data. We do not sell or “share” personal information as defined by the CCPA.

To exercise rights, contact us at [privacy@betternightsapp.com].

7. Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Security

We use technical and organizational measures appropriate to the risk (including encryption in transit via HTTPS and access controls). However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9. International Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for cross-border transfers.

10. Third-Party Links

The app may link to third-party sites or services. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you in the app or by other appropriate means. The revised policy will be effective when posted with the “Last updated” date.
